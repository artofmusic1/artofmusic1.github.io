About us

about us

I am Raghunandan M.S. the Founder of the School. I was a veena learner and started my primary days with Vidhushi BALA SARASWATHI, and Continued junior lessons with Vid T.V.Mutthachar at Basavanagudi Bangaluru.

Art of Music is trying to provide the best platform for all the Music learners. We are providing a hygienic & healthy school environment . We even provide the musical instruments & study materials for the beginners. We have Flexible batches & timings throughout the week.

We are offering the service of the Musical instruments where every Musician will require . We do repairs of all electronic & acoustic instruments .We do have sales of musical intruments from a genuine channel getting the best product at a challenging price with warranty for all range.
